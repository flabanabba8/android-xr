# Source: Android XR Developer Catalyst Program

**Source:** developer.android.com/develop/xr/catalyst  
**Fetched:** 2026-05-21  

## Key Takeaways

1. **Non-recoupable grants** at multiple funding levels, milestone-based distribution
2. **Hardware dev kits** for wired XR glasses, audio glasses, display glasses
3. **Deadline: June 30, 2026** — notification by July 15
4. **Developers retain full IP** — no rights transfer
5. **7 priority verticals**: media, gaming, productivity, navigation, social, health, commerce
6. **Eligibility**: must plan to publish within 6-12 months; hardware ships to US, Japan, UK, EU
7. **Selection criteria**: vertical alignment, developer readiness, funding articulation, innovation
