# Android XR Developer Catalyst Program

Google's grant program providing funding, hardware dev kits, and technical support for Android XR developers.

## What You Get

- **Non-recoupable grants** — multiple funding levels based on need, milestone-based distribution
- **Hardware dev kits** — wired XR glasses, audio glasses, display glasses
- **Technical support** — specialized channels, help preparing for Google Play
- **Full IP ownership** — developers retain all intellectual property

## Timeline

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2026-05-19 | Applications open |
| **2026-06-30** | **Application deadline (11:59 PM PDT)** |
| 2026-07-15 | Selection notification |
| Post-selection | Onboarding: sign agreements, receive dev kits |

## Eligibility

- Must plan to publish on Android XR within **6-12 months**
- Hardware ships to: US, Japan, UK, EU
- Can build with: Kotlin/Jetpack XR SDK, Unity, Godot, or Unreal Engine

## Priority Verticals

Media & Entertainment, Gaming, Productivity & Learning, Discovery & Navigation, Messaging & Social, Health & Wellness, Commerce & Payments. Other verticals welcome if clear XR use case.

## Selection Criteria

- Vertical alignment with priority areas
- Developer readiness (team, skills, timeline)
- Clear articulation of funding requirements
- Innovation and market potential

## Grant Milestones

Funding distributed at key milestones:
1. Signed program agreements
2. Technical design completion
3. Publishing on Google Play
