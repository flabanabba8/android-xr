# Google Play for XR

Android XR apps are distributed through Google Play with XR-specific quality guidelines.

## Quality Requirements

- Must meet Android XR quality guidelines (details TBD as SDK matures)
- Standard Google Play policies apply
- XR-specific packaging requirements for spatial content

## Catalyst Milestone

Publishing on Google Play is the final milestone for [[catalyst-program]] grant distribution.
