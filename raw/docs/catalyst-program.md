# Android XR Developer Catalyst Program

Fetched from: https://developer.android.com/develop/xr/catalyst
Date: 2026-05-21

## Program Overview

Empower developers with resources, hardware, and funding to build and scale XR experiences for wired XR glasses and intelligent eyewear.

## What's Included

- Non-recoupable grants at different funding levels (milestone-based)
- Hardware dev kits: wired XR glasses (XREAL Project Aura), audio glasses, display glasses
- Specialized technical support
- Resources for Google Play preparation

## Timeline

- Applications open: May 19, 2026
- Application deadline: June 30, 2026 (11:59 PM PDT)
- Selection notification: July 15, 2026
- Post-selection: onboarding, agreements, dev kit distribution

## Eligibility

- Must publish on Android XR within 6-12 months
- Hardware ships to: US, Japan, UK, EU
- Can build with: Kotlin/Jetpack XR SDK, Unity, Godot, Unreal Engine

## Priority Verticals

Media & Entertainment, Gaming, Productivity & Learning, Discovery & Navigation, Messaging & Social, Health & Wellness, Commerce & Payments

## Selection Criteria

- Vertical alignment
- Developer readiness
- Clear articulation of funding requirements
- Innovation and market potential

## IP

Developers retain full ownership of their intellectual property.

## Grant Milestones

1. Signed program agreements
2. Technical design completion
3. Publishing in Google Play
